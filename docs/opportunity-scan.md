# Lab 02 Opportunity Scanning

## Ideas Reviewed from Lab 1

| Idea | Problem Area | Target User | Current Alternative | Initial Technology Direction |
|------|-------------|------------|--------------------|-----------------------------|
| AI Assistant Tool for IT Students | Difficulty understanding coding and technical concepts | IT and Computer Science Students | Google, Stack Overflow, generic AI chatbots, classmates | Web-based AI learning assistant |
| Student Apartment Finder Platform | Difficulty finding apartments near campus | University students, especially newcomers | Facebook groups, agents, seniors, word of mouth | Apartment finder chatbot and listing platform |
| Campus Student Marketplace | Difficulty buying and selling second-hand items | University students | Facebook groups, notice boards, word of mouth | Campus-only marketplace platform |

## Opportunity Discovery Table

| No. | Observed Problem | Target User | Current Alternative | Possible IT Solution | Feasible Technology |
|------|-----------------|------------|--------------------|---------------------|--------------------|
| 1 | IT students struggle with coding and debugging | IT students | Google, Stack Overflow, ChatGPT | AI Assistant for IT Students | HTML/CSS/JS + AI API |

| 4 | Students miss assignment deadlines | University students | Manual reminders | Deadline Reminder Dashboard | Google Sheets Dashboard |
